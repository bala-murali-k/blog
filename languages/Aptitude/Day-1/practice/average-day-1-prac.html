  1. Problem 1:

    The average of five consecutive numbers is 27. What is the largest of these numbers?

    1. 29
    2. 30
    3. 28
    4. 27

    Answer :

    Here we are going to use the formula Average = sum of values / total no. of values
    Step 1: ( (x-2) + (x-1) + x + (x+1) + (x+2) ) / 5 = 27
    Step 2: 5x = 27 * 5
    Step 3: 5x = 135
    Step 4: 5x / 5 = 135 / 5 (divide both sides by 5 to find x)
    Step 5: x = 27
    Step 6: 27 + 2 = 29 (The largest number)
    The correct answer is 29.


  2. Problem 2:

    The average of 30 students in a class is 15 years. If the age of the teacher is included, the average becomes 16 years. what is the age of the teacher?

    1. 45 years.
    2. 46 years.
    3. 47 years.
    4. 48 years.

    Here we are going to use the formula Average = sum of values / total no. of values
    Step 1: sum of 30 students = 30 * 15
    Step 2: sum 0f 30 students = 450
    Step 3: sum of students with teacher = 31 * 16
    Step 4: sum of students with teacher = 496
    Step 5: age of the teacher = 496 - 450
    Step 6: age of the teacher is 46
    The correct answer is 46 years.


  3. Problem 3:

    The average of 20 numbers is 0 of them, atmost how many of them may be greater than zero?

    1. 19
    2. 20
    3. 10
    4. 0

    Note: The word atmost means the possible maximum value.
    Explanation: Here the given problem is, what is the maximum numbers can be positive also the average of the numbers also need to be zero.
    Basically the average is the total value of numbers divided by the total no of values. The average will be zero only if the total value of numbers is zero.
    Let's understand this problem with 5 numbers. The goal is to make the average 0.
    Step 1: Average = ( 500 + 500 + 500 + 500 + (-2000) ) / 5
    Step 2: Average = 0 / 5
    Step 3: Average = 0
    so now for 20 numbers the atmost positive values can be 19.
    The correct answer is 19.


  4. Problem 4:

    The average of 4 numbers is 20, and the average of 6 other numbers is 30. What is the average of all 10 numbers?

    1. 25
    2. 26
    3. 27
    4. 28

    Here we are going to use the formula Average = sum of values / total no. of values
    Step 1: sum of values 1 = 4 * 20
    Step 2: sum of values 1 = 80
    Step 3: sum of values 2 = 6 * 30
    Step 4: sum of values 2 = 180
    Step 5: Total average = (80 + 180) / 10
    Step 6: Total average = 260 / 10
    Step 7: Average = 26
    The answer is 26.

    1. 105
